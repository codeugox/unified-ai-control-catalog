# Unified AI Control Catalog

Assessor-oriented AI governance controls, evidence expectations,
confidence-labeled crosswalks, and governance-as-code artifacts.

UACC is for AI governance, security, risk, compliance, audit, and model
risk teams that need controls they can assess — not just principle-level
alignment.

!!! warning "Working reference, not compliance"
    UACC does not provide legal advice, certify compliance, replace conformity
    assessment, or create a regulatory safe harbor.
