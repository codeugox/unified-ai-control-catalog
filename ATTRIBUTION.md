## Related academic work

UACC is informed by related academic work on translating AI risk-management guidance into assessable control structures. In particular, the Lifecycle-Integrated AI Governance Control Framework (LIAGCF) paper is relevant because it evaluates a control-based approach that aligns AI governance controls to NIST AI RMF functions and NIST SP 800-53 control families within cybersecurity GRC programs.
